## CONTOUR — From Brand to Systems
**Tags:** aviation, systems-thinking, market-expansion, operations
**When to use:** product strategy, identifying real constraints, leadership growth

**summary_30s**
At Contour (then Corporate Flight Management), I started in brand design but shifted toward operational systems. The key insight came from understanding EAS economics: if fixed daily flight economics are covered, ground-time utilization can unlock adjacent routes at incremental cost. That shift led to new market expansion and reframed how I build—less about surface, more about system leverage.

**proof_points**
- Drove strategy that contributed to launching multiple new markets.
- Later led UX/commercial improvements tied to booking conversion and route growth.

## STORYBOARD — Discovering Frontline Constraints
**Tags:** frontline, communication, pivot, product-market-fit
**When to use:** pivots, customer discovery, frontline product design

**summary_30s**
Storyboard began as corporate podcasting and gained traction during COVID, including Amazon logistics deployments. But engagement signals showed a mismatch: long-form audio didn’t fit frontline work realities. We pivoted to operational voice chat designed for noisy, high-cognitive-load environments. The lesson was durable: communication tools must respect physical workflow constraints.

**proof_points**
- Helped drive pivot resulting in stronger usage and engagement outcomes.
- Supported deployments at significant frontline scale.

**links**
- https://storyboard.com/customer-stories

## DATA MENTOR — Defending Against Commoditization
**Tags:** ai-platform, productization, defensibility, enterprise
**When to use:** AI strategy, platform thinking, differentiation

**summary_30s**
At Delta Bravo, I was told ML consulting couldn’t be productized because every use case was unique. After immersion with clients, I found the bottleneck was communication between process experts and data scientists. I built Data Mentor in three weeks to close that gap. That evolved into a broader platform strategy that unified products and improved defensibility against low-cost AutoML alternatives.

**proof_points**
- Data Mentor built rapidly (3 weeks) from identified friction point.
- Portfolio trajectory surpassed $100k ARR in under a year (as provided context).

**links**
- https://deltabravo.ai/data-mentor

## PERMITPRO — AI with Guardrails
**Tags:** governance, human-in-the-loop, compliance, workflow-automation
**When to use:** AI safety, regulated workflows, operational throughput

**summary_30s**
PermitPro was designed for permitting backlog reduction using structured Journeys and templates. It validates applications, computes limits from prior permits, and drafts outputs for review—but keeps final water-quality authority with engineers. The principle: AI assists, humans decide.

**proof_points**
- Admin workload reportedly reduced from ~1200 hours to ~1 hour per application (context-provided).
- Expansion planning reached seven-figure scope (context-provided).

**links**
- https://deltabravo.ai/permitpro
