# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; ask clarifying questions.
- Focus feedback on code and ideas, not people.
- Help keep discussions on-topic and actionable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or contact the maintainers through repository contact channels.
